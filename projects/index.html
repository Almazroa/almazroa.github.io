Project Boredom

I have 6 months to reach a state of boredom.

This was an experiment originating from my belief that peak creativity comes from boredom. We know the proverb "Necessity is the mother of invention," and that boredom is the lack of interest, stimulation, challenge, etc.

Results: Fail. I always found something to do. New hypothesis: I was going through rapid cycles of boredom and finding something to do.
